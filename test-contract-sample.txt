FREELANCE SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Freelance Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of January 15, 2024, by and between TechCorp Solutions Inc. ("Client") and John Developer ("Freelancer").

1. SERVICES
Freelancer agrees to provide web development services including frontend development, backend API integration, and database design as outlined in the attached Statement of Work.

2. PAYMENT TERMS
Client shall pay Freelancer $8,000 per month, payable within 30 days of invoice. Late payments will incur a 6% penalty per month. All payments are non-refundable.

3. TERM AND TERMINATION
This Agreement shall commence on February 1, 2024 and continue for 6 months. Client may terminate this agreement at any time without notice. Freelancer must provide 60 days notice to terminate.

4. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
All work product, code, designs, and deliverables created by Freelancer shall immediately become the exclusive property of Client upon creation, regardless of payment status.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY
Freelancer agrees to maintain strict confidentiality of all Client proprietary information. This obligation continues indefinitely after termination.

6. LIABILITY AND INDEMNIFICATION
Freelancer's liability is limited to $500 total. Freelancer makes no warranties of any kind. Freelancer shall indemnify and hold harmless Client from any and all claims, including attorney fees.

7. NON-COMPETE
Freelancer agrees not to work for any competing business for 2 years after this agreement ends, within a 500-mile radius.

8. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of California. Any disputes must be resolved in California courts.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement.
